Mentorship Infrastructure
for the Space & Satellite Industry

Founding Partner Invitation

SSPI is building the first industry-wide architecture to support, develop, and recognize the mentors who strengthen the space and satellite talent pipeline. This is not a matching program. It is shared infrastructure that transitions mentorship from an informal practice to a formal, portable professional credential shaped by diverse industry leaders.

One Credential
The standard is the same regardless of context. Curriculum may vary. The credential belongs to the mentor and travels with them.
Practice, Not Milestone
Maintaining the credential requires ongoing participation. Mentorship is something you continue, not something you complete.
Recognition Follows Practice
