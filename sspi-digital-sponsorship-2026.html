Digital and Online Sponsorship Opportunities
2026

SSPI provides digital and online sponsorship opportunities in support of our two mission branches: Better Satellite World and Space and Satellite Futures. Companies can leverage these opportunities to demonstrate thought leadership, reach the next generation of space and satellite professionals, and contribute to building the industry's workforce and telling its best story.

Campaign rates vs. standalone rates: Components purchased as part of a Better Satellite World multi-platform campaign are available at campaign rates. Components purchased outside a campaign are priced at standalone rates. All sponsorships include usage rights — the produced asset is yours to use beyond the campaign.
Corporate Member Benefit
SSPI Corporate Members receive 30% off all sponsorship rates — campaign components and standalone sponsorships alike. Corporate Membership starts at $1,095/year.
